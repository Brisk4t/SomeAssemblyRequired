A Vite-built camera page that teaches gestures in the frame.

The left side hosts a live host camera feed with overlay cards that change as the user scrolls. The right side behaves like a premium product page, using soft pastel accents and a dark, glassy material look.

Step 01

Open palm

Spread your fingers and hold the palm fully visible inside the overlay box.

Step 02

Pinch

Bring thumb and index finger together until the tips nearly touch.

Step 03

Thumbs up

Raise the thumb while the other fingers curl down to finish the sequence.

Working behavior

Camera access uses the host browser and the gesture logic runs locally in the page. If permission is blocked, the UI shows a clear fallback state.
